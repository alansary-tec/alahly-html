ALAHLY CLUB

he club was founded by Omar Lotfy in 1907 and is among the most decorated in world football. Domestically, Al Ahly have a record of 44 Egyptian Premier League

  • Egyptian Premier league:44
  • Egyptian cup:39
  • super cup:15

The idea of establishing Al Ahly came in the first 10 years of the 20th century and was firstly raised by Omar Lotfy who was a student in the Egyptian Law School during his presidency of the High School Students Club, which was established in 1905. The establishment of a club for high school students was for political reasons, and the students needed a sports club to gather them for leisure and exercise. He discussed the idea of establishing the club with a group of friends who were enthusiastic about it, and in 1907 Al Ahly was established.[3]
